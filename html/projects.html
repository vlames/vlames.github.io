My Projects

I worked on the project many years ago. At that time I worked on the quiz creator terminal application. I thought of descovering how the pretty apple icons are made. I did a research and found steps to follow and do just that. The most difficult part was to design the icon itself. I used some graphical application (could have been photoshop) to assemble different pre-made parts and add my touch. Because the application was called the Quiz Creator, I thought it would be a good idea to unite a few concepts involved in it: questions that make up a quiz and building.

This was one of the first, outside of class projects. One day a person spoke to me. He recently arrived to US and was studying to become a truck driver. He wanted to use an existing quiz software, add questions to it and study. At that time I was at the beginning of my programming education and figured it would be nearly impossible for me to add questions to and existing software if I don't know how to code on that level. I decided to create my own, simple application, which turned out to be complex, but I enjoyed the thrill of developing it.

SubOps is one of my recent projects. I wanted to learn how to wrie subtitles and help out with translating spiritually important videos to my native language. Writing subtitles is not a so easy task as one might think. Besides content, it involves timing for each sibtitle line. I ended up using the Audacity software to view audio waves in a particular video. The problem was that the program has so called labels which do give start and end time but formatted differently. Because of that I wrote one of the features that would convert label time format to SRT.
