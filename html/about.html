Origins

I was born in Bryansk, Russia. I have brothers and sisters who live in different parts of the world. Due to the type of job my dad had, our family moved frequently. We lived in Bryansk, Kaluga, Novaya Usman, and Voronezh. In my home country, I completed 7 grades of general education and 5 years of music education. In music school I learned how to play piano. At the end of 2005, my family moved to United States.

USA

At the time of my arival to US, I was 15. I knew only a few words in English and was not able to communicate. ESL program in Milwaukie and David Douglas High Schools helped me learn basics of English and move on to higher education. Originally, I thought of studing to become an Ophthalmologist, an eye doctor, but at one point decided to explore programming. Here, in US, I attended a concert in which one of the people played an instrument called pan flute. I was able to purchase it in Hawaii and grasp the basics of it.

Now

Today, I learn new programing languages and aquire skills to become a better engineer. I continue to play piano and a pan flute in church. I am a member of international choir group, Oregon Adventist Men's Choir. We hold concerts in Oregon, Washington, and other countries and strive to bring a positive change in society.
